Frequently Asked Questions

What is LoomPath?
LoomPath is a non-profit initiative focused on expanding access to STEM education through hands-on workshops, mentorship, and community-led programs.
Who can participate?
Students of all backgrounds and experience levels are welcome. Our workshops are designed to be inclusive and adaptable to different classrooms and communities.
How can I volunteer or join the team?
Visit our Get Involved page to explore roles and submit an application through our Google Form. Selected applicants may be invited for a short interview and training.
How are donations used?
Donations directly support workshop kits, materials, training resources, outreach efforts, and access for underserved schools and communities.
Where are you based?
LoomPath operates globally through volunteers and chapters, with core coordination based in Indonesia.
How can my school or organization partner with LoomPath?
Schools and NGOs can collaborate with LoomPath by co-hosting workshops, supporting outreach, or integrating our programs into existing initiatives. Contact us at loompath.id@gmail.com to start a conversation.
