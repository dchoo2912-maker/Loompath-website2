Get Involved

Volunteer, take on a leadership role, or bring LoomPath to your school.

Mentorship Team

Lead Mentor – Runs breakout rooms and leads concept-based teaching.

Curriculum & Activity Lead – Develops activity designs, prepares scientific explanations, creates slides and demonstrations, tests experiments, and ensures accuracy and clarity.

Outreach Team

Social Media Lead – Designs posts, manages accounts, and handles content scheduling.

Communications Lead – Writes announcements, oversees email output, and maintains consistent messaging.

Partnerships Lead – Initiates collaborations with schools, clubs, and organizations.

Events & Promotion Lead – Leads event planning with partner schools or organizations and coordinates promotion.

Operations Team

Workshop Logistics Lead – Manages schedules, links, registrations, and attendance.

Materials & Prep Lead – Finalizes materials, prep guides, and technical requirements.

Data & Feedback Lead – Collects feedback, tracks metrics, and maintains participation data.

Volunteer

Support workshops, help guide activities, create content, or contribute to operations and outreach. Roles include:

  • Mentorship & breakout guidance
  • Activity design & testing
  • Social media & communications
  • Partnerships & outreach
  • Logistics, materials, and data
  • Media & editing
  • Operations support

No prior experience required. Training is provided.

Start a Chapter

Run LoomPath activities at your school or community. You’ll receive:

  • Full session plans and guides
  • Recorded demos and training
  • Support with logistics and outreach
  • Access to new cycles and materials

Partner With Us

Schools and NGOs can collaborate by co-hosting sessions, supporting outreach, or integrating our workshops into their programs.

How to Apply

  1. Review the Roles — View all volunteer and leadership roles here
  2. Submit the Application Form — Google Form
  3. Short interview (for mentorship or chapter leadership roles)
  4. Attend training
  5. Join the team
