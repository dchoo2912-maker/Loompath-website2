Founder

Aaren Bhandari — Founder & CEO

Aaren founded LoomPath to turn curiosity into opportunity. He leads the development of STEM workshops, online programs, and student-led collaboration systems that make engineering concepts accessible to young learners.

His background in advanced science coursework and competitive STEM activities shaped LoomPath’s focus on clarity, hands-on exploration, and real-world problem-solving.

Aaren built LoomPath with the goal of lowering barriers for students who rarely get hands-on STEM exposure, believing early engineering experiences can build confidence and open long-term pathways in STEM.

Our Story

LoomPath began with a simple idea: STEM learning should be hands-on, creative, and open to everyone.

What started as small in-person workshops evolved into a global initiative delivering guided builds, concept lessons, and mini-projects accessible anywhere in the world.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make STEM education accessible, engaging, and empowering through hands-on learning and clear instruction.

We aim to help students see themselves as makers, engineers, and problem-solvers capable of shaping their future.
